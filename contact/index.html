Take a Meeting

No pitch. No proposal. A direct conversation with the leadership team about the real question underneath the stated problem.

Three ways to begin

Choose the format that fits the question.

Option A · Most begin here
A structured conversation
A direct meeting with The Tenth Floor leadership team. No pitch. No proposal. Within 24 hours of the meeting, a one-page written summary of what was heard is delivered. That summary belongs entirely to the organisation, regardless of what comes next. Most engagements begin from this conversation. Many do not progress beyond it, and that is the right outcome when the situation does not call for an engagement.
Email Hasnah →
Option B
A diagnostic sprint
A short, fixed-scope, fixed-fee investigation, scoped to a single specific question. One actionable, evidence-backed finding, built from the data and signals the organisation already has. Delivered within a few weeks. The finding, the evidence underneath it, and the working method belong fully to the organisation when the sprint concludes.
Discuss a sprint →
Option C
A full engagement
A consulting or people-development engagement with defined scope, fixed fee, and clear deliverables. The work concludes with a working method transferred to the client’s team, who can then apply the same approach to the next question independently. The capability stays inside the organisation. The reliance does not need to continue.
